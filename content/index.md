# Alissars Almanach

>[!tip] **A Mad Wizard's Folly**
>A wizard once battled a spider  
>And threw at her a Great Ball of Fire.  
>But the spider was tough and resistant.  
>The wizard grew apprehensive and distant.  
>So that day he simply chose to retire.

Well met, honoured traveller and welcome to a cowardly wizards sanctuary. After my short, inspired but not very fruitful, attempt at living the wholesome life of an adventurer, I, Alissar Fenric, have decided to retire early from adventuring life. Instead I will dedicate my time to the collection of stories of many a group of heroes that fared better and may yet lived longer than my own foolish attempts at bravery.

In these here pages you will find my ongoing efforts at chronicling and recording the heroic deeds of many such groups and all the folklore and stories that surround their valiant deeds and travels. The notes are neither complete, nor do they attempt to provide all side of any which story (whoever thinks they could), but they will grow at the very rate that my enthusiasm for such tales allows. Enjoy!
